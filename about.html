About Us

Our Story

Grace Community Church has been a beacon of faith and hope in our community for over 25 years. Founded with a vision to create a welcoming space for all people to encounter God's love, we have grown into a diverse and vibrant congregation.

Our journey began with a small group of believers who were passionate about making a difference. Today, we continue that mission with the same dedication and love that inspired our founding members.

Our Mission

We exist to help people discover and develop their relationship with God. Through worship, teaching, fellowship, and service, we aim to build a community that reflects Christ's love to the world.

We believe that every person has inherent value and a unique purpose. Our goal is to help individuals find their place in God's story and live out their calling with passion and purpose.
