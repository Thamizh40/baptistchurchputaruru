About Us

Our Story

Putaruru Baptist Church began as an outpost of the Morrinsville Baptist Church in 1929. A Sunday School was established in 1933. The Church was officially formed on 19 September, 1934, with 11 foundation members. The Church has been fulfilling its mission ever since, bringing the hope and good news of the gospel to the local community, and establishing an outpost in Rotorua in early 1949, as well as helping to establish Tokoroa Baptist Church at a later date. We are a charismatic, spirit filled, multi-cultural, multi-generational family of God.

Our Mission

Loving our community and introducing it to the hope we have in Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

That all will come to know the saving power of Jesus Christ and the love of God the Father, and the fellowship of the Holy Spirit.

Putaruru Baptist Church

A place of belonging, worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth.
