Our Services

Sunday Worship

Join us for inspiring worship services featuring contemporary music, powerful biblical teaching, and meaningful fellowship. Services at 10:30 AM

Construction Zone

Dynamic programs for teens and young adults to grow in faith, build friendships, and discover their purpose through engaging activities and mentorship. For more details please contact the Church office.

God Squad

A Sunday school programme for children aged 5 – 12 years operates during the Sunday morning service, giving our children a strong Biblical foundation.

Life Groups

There are various groups that meet throughout the week. Please contact the Church office to find one that suits you.

Children's Ministry

Safe, fun, and engaging programs for children from nursery through elementary school, teaching biblical values through creative activities and stories.

Prayer Ministry

Dedicated prayer meetings are held at the Church Thursdays and Sundays at 10am. There is also a prayer chain functioning in the church. Matthew 21:13a “And He declared to them, ‘It is written: My house will be called a house of prayer.
