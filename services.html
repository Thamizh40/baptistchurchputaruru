Our Services

Sunday Worship

Join us for inspiring worship services featuring contemporary music, powerful biblical teaching, and meaningful fellowship. Services at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM.

Youth Ministry

Dynamic programs for teens and young adults to grow in faith, build friendships, and discover their purpose through engaging activities and mentorship.

Community Outreach

Making a tangible difference through food drives, charity events, and volunteer opportunities that serve those in need within our community.

Bible Study

Weekly small group sessions that explore scripture in depth, foster meaningful discussions, and help apply biblical wisdom to everyday life.

Children's Ministry

Safe, fun, and engaging programs for children from nursery through elementary school, teaching biblical values through creative activities and stories.

Prayer Ministry

Dedicated prayer teams available to pray with you, provide spiritual support during challenges, and celebrate your victories in faith.
